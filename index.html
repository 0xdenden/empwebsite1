FEATURED: Porsche 911 Carrera 4S

The Porsche 911 Carrera 4S is a high-performance all-wheel-drive sports car that features a rear-mounted 3.0-liter twin-turbo flat-6 engine, and a top speed of 308 km/h. It is visually distinct from the two-wheel-drive models with its wider rear body and a full-width rear light bar. The all-wheel-drive system provides enhanced grip and stability, especially in challenging conditions, while still offering a dynamic and engaging driving experience.
